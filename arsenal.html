Bowling Arsenal

The following is a list of all of my owned bowling balls, and my performance with each ball. The Ebonite Maxim was my first bowling ball I got on my birthday in 2006. The Storm Hy-Road Pearl was a ball that I received when a friend of mine retired it in 2012, and has been my strike ball from that point forward. I also received a Storm Hy-Road Solid a few years after that, but that bowl never made it into League play. I used it in practice a few times, but I couldn't get used to the way I needed to throw it. In 2016 I received the DV8 Deviant when I completed the Rowdy Rollers league.
